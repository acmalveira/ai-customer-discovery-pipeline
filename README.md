# AI Customer Discovery Pipeline

An AI-supported customer discovery and lead qualification pipeline designed to support decision-making in web development projects.

## Overview

This repository contains the research artifacts, datasets, questionnaires, documentation, and replication materials associated with the development and evaluation of an AI-supported customer discovery and lead qualification pipeline.

The proposed pipeline combines conversational AI, lead scoring mechanisms, and workflow automation to support customer discovery, requirement elicitation, and decision-making during early-stage interactions between web development professionals and prospective customers.

## Research Context

Customer discovery and lead qualification are critical activities in web development projects. However, these processes are often informal, highly dependent on subjective judgment, and frequently performed with incomplete information.

This research investigates how artificial intelligence can support these activities through a human-AI decision support approach.

## Research Question

How does an AI-supported customer discovery and lead qualification pipeline influence designers' decision-making processes during early-stage customer interactions?

## Proposed Pipeline

The pipeline integrates:

* Conversational AI agents for customer discovery;
* Requirement elicitation support;
* Lead qualification based on an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP);
* Automated workflow orchestration;
* AI-generated diagnostic artifacts and website concepts.

The objective is to improve:

* Decision confidence;
* Requirement clarity;
* Customer understanding;
* Consistency in lead qualification.
